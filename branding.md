# AWG ACTIVE CONSULTING - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

AWG ACTIVE CONSULTING is a financial consulting firm with the tagline "Let the money flow" that emphasizes precision, authority, and professional financial expertise.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Golden Yellow**: `#FCC900`
  - Used for primary branding, logos, and key call-to-action elements
  - Represents energy, optimism, and financial success
  
- **Black**: `#000000`
  - Used for text, backgrounds, and creating strong contrast
  - Conveys authority, professionalism, and trust

### Accent Color
- **Light Gray**: `#F5F2E9`
  - Used for subtle backgrounds and secondary elements
  - Provides visual breathing room and elegant contrast

## Typography

### Primary Fonts
- **Coco Gothic**: Modern, clean sans-serif for headings and branding
- **Open Sans**: Readable, professional font for body text and general content

### Font Usage Guidelines
- Coco Gothic for headers, navigation, and brand elements
- Open Sans for paragraphs, descriptions, and general content

## Logo Guidelines

### Logo Elements
- **AWG** letters in golden yellow (#FCC900)
- **ACTIVE CONSULTING** text in matching brand colors
- **Tagline**: "Let the money flow" positioned consistently with the logo

### Logo Variations
- Light background: Golden yellow logo with black text
- Dark background: Golden yellow logo with white/light text
- Single color: All black version for specific use cases

## Brand Personality

### Core Values
- **Precision**: Measured approach to fiscal-financial strategy
- **Authority**: Professional expertise in HR and GDPR compliance
- **Efficiency**: Streamlined processes and attention to detail
- **Trustworthiness**: Reliable partnership for financial growth

### Visual Style
- High contrast color combinations
- Clean, modern design aesthetic
- Professional business presentation
- Bold, confident visual hierarchy

## Implementation Notes

This brand guide ensures consistent visual identity across all digital and print materials, reinforcing AWG's position as a trusted financial consulting partner.
